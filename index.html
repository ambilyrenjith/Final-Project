Welcome to Michael's Furniture Store

Michael's Furniture Store has been furnishing homes across California for over 20 years. We're a locally-owned, third-generation family business. We'd like to share with you what we know about creating a comfortable, stylish environment for you and your family.

Our California home furniture stores have the furniture styles, designs, and quality you're looking for, whether it’s contemporary, modern, transitional or traditional. Find living room furniture, including sofas, loveseats, sectionals and more to create a welcoming space. Seat your guests in comfort with our wide selection of dining room furniture. Create a backdrop for the perfect night’s sleep with our master bedroom collections or outfit your child’s room or guest room in styles made to last. If you're not sleeping as well as you once were, it may be time for a new mattress. Our sleep specialists will help you find the mattress that’s right for you. Whether you work from home or just need a well-organized workspace, our office furniture will help you get down to business with a space you'll be delighted to work in.

Where you shop matters. That’s why we have a no-haggle pricing policy , a huge selection, and a no-pressure sales environment. There are no gimmicks, no smoke and mirrors, and no phony sales — just quality, affordable furniture. We simply aren't like anyone else. We call it the Jerome’s Way.
